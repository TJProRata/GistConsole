Woman's World Answers

AI-Powered Health Q&A

Welcome to the Women's World Answers widget demo! This page demonstrates the floating widget embedded on a real webpage. The widget appears as a button in the bottom-right corner of your screen.

How It Works

  • Click the floating button in the bottom-right corner
  • Browse seed questions or type your own health question
  • Get instant answers powered by AI
  • Explore related health topics and wellness tips
Pro Tip: Try clicking one of the seed question pills to auto-populate the search input with common health questions!

Widget Features

The Women's World widget is designed for health and wellness publishers who want to provide instant answers to their readers' questions:

  • Glassmorphism design with smooth animations
  • Dual-carousel seed questions (health + wellness topics)
  • Auto-scroll with hover-to-pause functionality
  • Responsive design optimized for mobile and desktop
  • Customizable colors, placement, and branding
  • Optional OpenAI streaming for real-time answers

Integration

Embedding this widget on your site is as simple as adding three lines of code:

<script src="https://wwwidget.vercel.app/womens-world-floating.js"></script>
<div id="womens-world-widget"></div>
<script>GistWidget.init({...});</script>
